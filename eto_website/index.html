UDAAN

The Employement and training office of JCBOSEUST, Faridabad

J. C. Bose University has a wonderful legacy of over 50 years and it has been consistently contributing towards creation of competent engineering professionals and entrepreneurs. We boasts of very rich alumni and we are eager to serve the ever-evolving needs of organizations across different industries and scale by offering the excellent human capital we have nurtured over the years. Please feel free to reach out to us for any necessary support or queries. The dedicated UDAAN - Employement and Training Office at JC Bose UST is fully-equipped to help create the right match between organizations’ unique talent needs and the aspirations borne by our graduating students. We look forward to hear from you!
